About Me

Hi! I am a Ph.D student in molecular microbiology at Institut Pasteur de Côte d’Ivoire, in Abidjan. I was born in Lome, Togo were I did my primary school and college before going to Maroc for undergraduate studies. The amazing project on assessing the bacteriophages effectiveness against multiresistant bacteria sent me to Côte d’Ivoire.

Research Interest

I am particularly interrested in phages. Their biology, their evolution and their mechanisms. I am also interessted in using them as agent against multidrug resistant bacteria as part of phage therapies.
